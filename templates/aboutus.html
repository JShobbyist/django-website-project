We measure our success by your success. To be successful in the era of Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence - you need a partner that enables change by technology and sustains through capabilities, and we’ve got that down to a science.

Our purpose is to build analytics-driven organization by combining the latest techniques with deep industry, functional, and analytics expertise to help clients capture the most value from data.

What? At-scale profitability, powered by dependable and defendable analytics.

We focus our efforts within, and at the intersection of, three key domains: (1) Machine Learning and Microservices, (2) Finance, Treasury and Risk, and (3) Pricing and Profitability. Within, across and at the intersection of these domains, BBA's work focuses on analyzing customer behavior – helping clients develop a deeper understanding of customer attributes, decisions that they choose to make and their impact on risk, profitability and balance sheet.

How? By bringing analytics to life.

We meet our clients anywhere they are in their journey to become data-driven, implement the solution on the sandbox, get clients' buy-in and support 24X7.

Proprietary Database

Our large proprietary database includes dataset from banks, credit unions and other financial services firms from Americas, Europe, Middle East and Africa enabling us to directionally benchmark modelling parameters - derived based on internal data and the imact of outputs, by region, product line and institution type. Combined with in-depth knowledge of the structure and dynamic of the financial services sector, this allows us to formulate accurate modelling predictions.

  • 100+ risk, capital and profitability parameter benchmarks, covering time series and organized cross-sectionally
  • Over 300+ behavioral factors tracked for use in scoring, loss severity and profitability models
  • More than $1 trillion in assets modelled

Management

Sohail Farooq

Chief Executive Officer and Founder

Sohail Farooq

Chief Executive Officer and Founder

Based on a vision of pushing the cutting edge of advanced analytics to guide transformations and boost the effectiveness of strategic decision making, Sohail founded BBA - BankingBook Analytics in 2018. Recognized as a customer-oriented, results-driven business leader, his focus is on the development of domain expertise and the growth of the firm. Throughout his 20+ year career, Sohail has worked to adapt technology for the development of finance, risk and treasury functions at financial services organizations. He is passionate about helping small to mid-sized financial institutions close the technology gap. Prior to founding BBA, Sohail led and managed Toronto-based Nexx Consultants - a specialist risk and capital management advisory firm. Before Nexx, Sohail worked as Principal with Oliver Wyman and Sr. Director with Moody's Analytics. Sohail holds a M.Sc. in Quantitative Finance from Frankfurt School of Finance and Management, and a M.B.A. in Finance from Dalhousie University.
